# Masar — PRODUCT.md

## Register

Brand. This is the marketing/landing site for Masar; the design is the pitch.

## What it is

Masar ("مسار") is an internal municipality tool for road-damage management:
field employees scan/report damage (potholes, cracks, subsidence), supervisors
triage and dispatch on a live map, repair crews execute and document fixes.
One report, one path — بلاغٌ واحد، طريقٌ واحد.

## Audience

The municipality buyer (city operations directors, GIS/infrastructure leads)
and the field teams who will use it. The reporter is a **field employee, not a
citizen** — copy is framed for the municipality, not a consumer app store.
Arabic-first (RTL), Levant/Gulf municipal context.

## Purpose of this site

Convince a municipality to request a demo. The narrative walks the three
roles (scanner → dispatcher → repair crew) through one report's lifecycle,
shows the tech (AI damage detection), and lands on a demo CTA.

## Brand personality

Civic, capable, calm. Confident engineering, not startup hype. Warm enough
for field workers, credible enough for a director's procurement meeting.

## Visual system (locked)

- Palette: brand blues (peacock #34A8D8, horizon #197FB0, sports-teal #16668E)
  + semantic pipeline colors (informative blue = new report, notice amber =
  assigned, positive green = fixed, negative red).
- Type: Rubbama (display) + Almarai (body), Arabic-first.
- Surfaces: white/whitesmoke, 32px card radius, **no borders, no box shadows**
  (deliberate flat look — depth comes from color, motion, and layering).
- Motion: GSAP + ScrollTrigger + Lenis smooth scroll. Scroll-driven
  storytelling sections with static stacks for mobile and reduced motion.

## Anti-references

- Consumer fintech/app-store gloss ("download on the App Store" framing).
- Generic SaaS hero-metric templates, gradient text, glassmorphism-as-default.
- Government-portal dryness — the site should feel alive, not bureaucratic.
